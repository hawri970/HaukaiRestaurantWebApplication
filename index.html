Welcome to The Haukai

We love Aotearoa, it's the place we call home and we want to share all the flavours we love about our home with our guests.

Our menus are inspired by the whenua (land), moana (sea), and tāngata (people) of Aotearoa. We place a special focus on researching and showcasing Māori and Pasifika ingredients in modern and innovative ways.
