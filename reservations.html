Reservation FAQ

Cancellation Policy

For reservations of seven people or less, if you wish to cancel your reservation, we require 72 hours notice or a cancellation fee will apply. For groups reservations, that is eight people or more, we require at least seven days notice or a cancellation fee applies.

Dietary Requirements

The Haukai menu is based upon well known Māori and Pasifika concepts, legends and contemporary stories. As such, we try to make as few changes to the menu as possible so that guests can enjoy the menu as the Haukai team envisioned it. This means that we are not always able to make substitutions.

For all other dietary requirements and if you have any further questions regarding our dietary requirements policy, please email info@haukai.co.nz before making your booking so we can discuss the necessary arrangements.

Private Events and Larger Bookings

Haukai is available for private events outside of our normal operating hours. Please email info@haukai.co.nz for more information.
