Our Privacy Policy

Read our privacy policy here.
Please email info@haukai.co.nz or give us a call on 09 401 4019 if you have any questions.

We collect personal information from you, including your:

  • Name.
  • Contact Information (Email and Phone number).
  • Interactions with us.

We collect the information via:

  • Reservations page google form.

We collect your personal information to:

  • Confirm reservations.
  • Keep in contact with customers and inform them about news and deals from the Haukai Restaurant.
  • Gather analytical information about how the website is used to allow us to improve the usr experience.

Providing some information is optional.

  • If you choose not to enter name and contact information, we'll be unable to keep you up to date with updates from Haukai Restaurant or process any reservations online.
  • If you choose not to enter the ability to store cookies in your web browser we'll use analytics to improve the overall visitor experience.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@haukai.co.nz
